Welcome to the BIMEDA project


This is the website of a research project about the technical, social and ethical opportunities and challenges of big data use in primary care.

It is funded by the European Commission's Horizon 2020 - Research and Innovation Framework Programme (H2020-EU.1.3.2.), while the fellow (Dr Paraskevas Vezyridis) is hosted at the Centre for Health Innovation, Leadership and Learning (CHILL), University of Nottingham. The project will run from August 1, 2015 to July 31, 2017.

Keep checking these pages for regular updates and always feel free to drop a message with your comments/suggestions.
